### Scoring Capabilities
1. **Bouldering Comp**
   - Tracks up to 5 boulder routes
   - Includes flash bonus points
   - *Maximum score*: `14,240` points

2. **Mixed Comp**
   - Includes:
     * 2 rope routes
     * 2 boulder routes
     * 1 choice route
   - *Maximum score*: `9,931` points
